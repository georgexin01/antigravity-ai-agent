# AI Design Level & Quality Tracker

This artifact tracks the evolution of the AI agent's design capabilities across different projects. Each completed project is assigned a quality rating, which is compared to the previous project to measure improvement or regression.

## 1. Quality Rating Protocol

- **Base Level**: 1.0000
- **Rating Scale**: Decimal point tracking (+/- offsets like 0.001, 0.15, etc.)
- **Frequency**: Updated upon the first completion of every website or app project.
- **Reporting**: Current AI Design Level must be presented at the start of every new creation project.
