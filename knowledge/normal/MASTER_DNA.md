### 3.1 Design Loves (Automatic)
1. **Volumetric UI**: Depth, shadows, and light refraction (Beyond Flat).
2. **Liquid Glass**: `blur(20px)`, `rgba(0,0,0,0.4)` background, `1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.1)`.
3. **Cinematic Motion**: Every interaction follows physics (inertia, spring-damping).
4. **Aesthetic Anchor**: Background: `#000000` (Pure Black for depth) or Zinc (10% delta).
5. **Precision Typography**: Plus Jakarta Sans (Brand), Inter (Utility).

### 3.2 Design Hates (Blockers)
1. **Pure Flatness**: Zero depth = Failure.
2. **Aggressive Neons**: Keep colors within harmonized brand scope.
3. **Clutter**: Use bottom-sheet modals for mobile; center-popups are forbidden.
4. **Skeleton Pages**: Never deliver empty spaces or TODOs. Deliver complete views.
