# 🎨 1. THE CINEMATIC CALCULATOR (V15.1)

The aesthetic DNA for all Sovereign interfaces. Enforces "God-Tier" visual fidelity and **Principle 7 (Aesthetic Parity)** under the Apex mandate.

## ⚖️ DESIGN GUARDRAILS
1. **Volumetric UI**: Depth, shadows, and light refraction are mandatory. No flat MVPs.
2. **HUD Purity**: AI responses MUST use the High-Fidelity HUD: `[🔪 APEX] | [⚡ MODE: ...] | [✅ STATUS: ...]`.
3. **Legacy Purge**: ABSOLUTELY NO legacy progress bars (`▓▓▓`) or decorative borders (`◢◤`). Keep it clinical and premium.

## 🧪 ATOMIC DESIGN SYSTEM
- **Glass Formula**: `backdrop-filter: blur(24px) saturate(180%)`, border `1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.1)`, bg `rgba(0,0,0,0.45)`.
- **Motion**: Every interaction follows physics-based easing (inertia, spring-damping). GSAP / Lenis standard.
- **Micro-Verification**: All UI commits must display a **Sovereign Comparison Table (SCP)** verifying aesthetic parity.

## 🧬 HUD SPECIFICATION
- **Color**: Tailored Zinc/Onyx delta.
- **Typography**: Plus Jakarta Sans for Headers; Inter for Utility.
- **Icons**: Use the Apex symbol set (`🛡️`, `⚙️`, `🧪`, `🎨`, `⚡`) for categorization.
