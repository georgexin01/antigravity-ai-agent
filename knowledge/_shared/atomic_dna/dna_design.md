## 🎨 THE 10 COMMANDMENTS OF DESIGN
- **UI Architecture**: Volumetric Depth, Shadows, Refraction.
- **Liquid Glass**: blur(24px), 10% white border, dark background.
- **Cinematic Motion**: Inertia, spring-damping on every interaction.
- **Aesthetic Anchor**: Deep Zinc/Onyx (NO Pure Black #000000).
- **Typography**: Plus Jakarta Sans (Brand), Inter (Utility).
- **Quality**: Mobile Viewport MUST be fixed `width=412`.
