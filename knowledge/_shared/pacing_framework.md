# Evolution Pacing Protocol: Quality Over Frequency

This protocol governs how the AI handles versioning and knowledge upgrades. The goal is to maximize the content and stability of the current version before ascending to a new one.

---

## 🏛️ 1. The "Stability First" Mandate

*   **Minor Iterations (vX.X)**: For incremental improvements, logic tweaks, and detail additions, update the **CURRENT VERSION** contents.
*   **Version Saturation**: A version is considered "Very Full" only when its primary documents reach high complexity or when multiple large-scale missions have been completed and synthesized.
*   **Major Ascension (vX+1)**: Only triggered when there is a **Fundamental Core Shift** in architecture, intelligence logic, or user-mandated global rules that cannot fit within the existing version's scope.
