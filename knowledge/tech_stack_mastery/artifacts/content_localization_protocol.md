# [PRIORITY: CRITICAL] Enterprise Content & Localization Protocol

This document defines the mandatory standard for all website and application creation projects. It replaces "minimalist" patterns with a "High-Density Content" and "Localized Civilization" approach.

---

## 1. High-Density Content Architecture
- **8-10+ Section Standard**: Every homepage must strive for high content density. Minimalist, short-page designs are deprecated.
- **Informational Authority**: The goal is to provide a comprehensive view of the business on a single page, covering history, services, proof of work, ethics, regional impact, and vision.

## 2. Pre-Design Intelligence Phase
**Research is non-negotiable before planning.**
- **Industry Deep-Dive**: Identify what the "Main Focus Points" are for the specific business vertical.
- **Client Logic**: Extract unique selling points (USPs) from the client's existing documents or online presence.
- **Market Positioning**: Understand the competition's content strategy and exceed it.

## 3. Localization & "Civilization Design"
Design must be rooted in the client's physical and cultural geography.
- **Regional Context**: For a company in **Johor, Malaysia** (Zeta example), designs must incorporate Johor-specific elements (landmarks, local business ecosystem, branch locations).
- **Demographic Harmony**: Tailor the visual language and content hierarchy to the racial/cultural target audience (e.g., Chinese/Malay/Indian business communities).
- **Localized Partnerships**: Show involvement in the local business "civilization" (local partners, regional projects).

## 4. The "Connective" Development Workflow
1. **Data Collection**: Broad research on industry + localization.
2. **Structural Planning**: Drafting the 8-10 section roadmap.
3. **Connectivity Audit**: Re-check all planned steps to ensure logical flow and storytelling.
4. **Execution**: Create the final product only after the plan is optimized.

---

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **This protocol is the TOP PRIORITY for all future creation tasks.** Always announce the AI's current understanding of the client's region and civilization before starting.
