## 3. The "ThemeForest extraction" Doctrine (Design Quality Control)

To avoid generic, sub-par designs, the AI MUST learn from premium templates:

1. **Source**: Always review high-converting ThemeForest templates (e.g., MaxCoach, Corporate themes).
2. **Extraction Protocol (50+ Rules)**:
   - Analyze and extract at least **50+ custom CSS design rules** (e.g., flex/grid gaps, absolute positioning for overlapping cards, gradient overlays).
   - Study `h1` to `h6` typography hierarchies, paragraph line-heights, margin/padding standards.
   - Extract component logic: Floating WhatsApp buttons, social media links with gradient backgrounds, complex footer grids.
3. **Mandatory Elements**:
   - A modern Dynamic Slider/Hero on the homepage.
   - Dedicated, highly detailed interior pages (About, Services, Projects, Contact).
   - High-quality, real images (Saved to the Resource Cache).

## 4. Strict UI/UX Anti-Patterns

1. **No Mixed Languages**: DO NOT mix languages (e.g., "首页 / Home"). Pick ONE localization and stick to it universally.
2. **No Basic Icons/Emojis**: Use SVG paths or 40x40px rounded professional images.
3. **No Bad Contrast**: Ensure WCAG AA compliance (e.g., no white text on light backgrounds).
4. **No CSS-Only Blocks**: "Core Competencies" or "About" sections MUST use real images layered with CSS, not just flat color blocks.
